Loch Long: a Scottish fjord

It was again a day trip from Edinburgh. I walked with a friend the final part of the Three Lochs Way, which begins in Garelochhead on Gare Loch, passes Loch Long and finishes in Tarbet on Loch Lomond. Also, I realised that Loch Long is a fjord! Isn’t it surprising that we can do a day trip to a fjord from Edinburgh?
